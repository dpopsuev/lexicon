# Retrospective

**Core idea:** At regular intervals, the team reflects on how to become more effective, then tunes and adjusts its behavior accordingly. (Agile Principle #12)

## Common Formats

- **Start/Stop/Continue** — what to start doing, stop doing, keep doing
- **4Ls** — Liked, Learned, Lacked, Longed for
- **Sailboat** — wind (helps), anchor (hinders), rocks (risks), island (goal)
- **Mad/Sad/Glad** — emotional temperature check

## Output

- **Action items** — concrete changes for next iteration
- **Experiments** — try something new, measure result
- **Appreciations** — recognize what went well

## vs Post-Mortem

| Retrospective | Post-Mortem |
|---|---|
| Regular (every sprint) | After incidents |
| Forward-looking (improve) | Backward-looking (understand) |
| Process-focused | Event-focused |
