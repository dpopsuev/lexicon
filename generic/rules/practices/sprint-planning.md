# Sprint Planning

**Core idea:** At the start of each sprint, the team selects items from the backlog, discusses how to implement them, and commits to a sprint goal.

## Two Parts

1. **What** — Product Owner presents highest-priority items. Team selects what fits the sprint capacity.
2. **How** — Team discusses implementation approach, breaks items into tasks, identifies risks.

## Outputs

- Sprint Goal — one sentence describing the sprint's purpose
- Sprint Backlog — selected items + tasks
- Capacity commitment — team agrees this is achievable

## Djinn Alignment

- 2Sec runs sprint planning continuously (not once per sprint)
- Planning Pyramid = structured decomposition of the sprint goal
- Scoring = capacity estimation per DAG node
